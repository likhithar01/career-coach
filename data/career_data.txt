RESUME TIPS:
- Keep your resume to 1 page if you have less than 5 years of experience.
- Use action verbs like "built", "designed", "led", "improved".
- Always tailor your resume to the job description.
- Include measurable achievements e.g. "Increased sales by 30%".
- Avoid generic phrases like "team player" or "hard worker".

INTERVIEW TIPS:
- Research the company before the interview.
- Use the STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, Result.
- Prepare 3-5 questions to ask the interviewer.
- Practice common questions: "Tell me about yourself", "What are your weaknesses?".
- Send a thank-you email within 24 hours after the interview.

JOB SEARCH TIPS:
- Apply to jobs on LinkedIn, Naukri, Internshala, and company websites.
- Network actively — 70% of jobs are filled through referrals.
- Follow up on applications after 1 week.
- Customize your cover letter for each application.

CAREER PATHS IN TECH:
- Software Engineer: Learn DSA, system design, Python/Java.
- Data Scientist: Learn Python, ML, statistics, SQL.
- Product Manager: Learn user research, roadmapping, agile.
- DevOps Engineer: Learn Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD pipelines.

SALARY NEGOTIATION:
- Always negotiate — 70% of employers expect it.
- Research market salary on Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, AmbitionBox.
- Never give a number first — ask what the budget is.
- Negotiate benefits too: remote work, learning budget, extra leave.

SKILLS IN DEMAND (2024-2025):
- AI/ML, Prompt Engineering, LLMs
- Cloud: AWS, Azure, GCP
- Cybersecurity
- Full Stack Web Development
- Data Analytics

RESUME FORMATS:
- Chronological: Best for people with steady work history.
- Functional: Best for career changers or gaps in employment.
- Combination: Best for experienced professionals.
- Always use ATS-friendly formats — avoid tables, images, columns.
- Use PDF format when submitting resumes.

COVER LETTER TIPS:
- Keep it to 3 paragraphs: Why you, Why this company, Call to action.
- Never start with "I am applying for...".
- Mirror the job description language.
- Show enthusiasm for the company specifically.
- Keep it under 400 words.

LINKEDIN TIPS:
- Use a professional headshot — profiles with photos get 21x more views.
- Write a headline beyond just your job title.
- Add 5+ relevant skills and get endorsements.
- Post content regularly to increase visibility.
- Connect with recruiters in your target companies.
- Turn on "Open to Work" if actively job hunting.

COMMON INTERVIEW QUESTIONS & HOW TO ANSWER:
- "Tell me about yourself" → 2 min pitch: past, present, future.
- "Why do you want this job?" → Research + align with your goals.
- "What is your weakness?" → Real weakness + how you're improving.
- "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?" → Growth-focused answer.
- "Why are you leaving your current job?" → Stay positive, never badmouth.

FREELANCING & SIDE INCOME:
- Platforms: Upwork, Fiverr, Toptal, Freelancer.
- Best freelance skills: Web dev, Design, Writing, Data analysis, AI/ML.
- Start with lower rates to build reviews, then increase.
- Always have a contract and milestone-based payments.

HIGHER EDUCATION ADVICE:
- MBA is useful for management/consulting roles.
- MS in CS/Data Science is valuable for technical roles.
- Online certifications: Google, AWS, Coursera, Udemy are well-recognized.
- MOOCs can substitute degrees for many tech jobs today.

PRODUCTIVITY & CAREER GROWTH:
- Build a personal brand on LinkedIn and GitHub.
- Contribute to open source projects.
- Write technical blogs on Medium or Hashnode.
- Attend meetups, hackathons, and tech conferences.
- Find a mentor in your field.
- Review and update your resume every 6 months.

REMOTE WORK TIPS:
- Highlight remote work experience on your resume.
- Platforms for remote jobs: Remote.co, We Work Remotely, Remotive.
- Show self-management and communication skills in interviews.
- Have a professional background for video interviews.
